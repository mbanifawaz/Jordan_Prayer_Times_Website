Amazing Features

Everything you need to stay connected with your daily prayers

Accurate Prayer Times

Prayer times for all 34 Jordanian cities sourced directly from the official Ministry of Awqaf website. Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, and Sunrise times.

Smart Notifications

Customizable prayer alerts with 5 different beautiful Athan recitations. Works even when the app is closed or screen is locked.

Complete Quran

Full Holy Quran with beautiful Medina layout, audio playback with multiple reciters, translations, full-text search, bookmarks, and Tafsir.

Qibla Compass & Map

Interactive compass showing direction to Mecca with real-time GPS-based map view, rotating indicator, and haptic feedback alignment detection.

Bilingual Support

Full English and Arabic interface with automatic RTL layout, Arabic numerals for authentic experience, and Hijri calendar with Islamic month names.

Works Offline

Works without internet after initial setup. Monthly caching with 6-hour smart sync saves battery (96% more efficient).

Dark Mode

Beautiful Material Design 3 UI with dark mode OLED optimization, animated prayer cards with countdown timer, and clean intuitive layout.

Hijri Calendar

View the Islamic Hijri calendar alongside the Gregorian date with Islamic month names in both Arabic and English.

Wear OS App

Prayer times on your wrist. Glanceable next prayer countdown, full times screen, watch face complication, and persistent notifications — all on your smartwatch.
